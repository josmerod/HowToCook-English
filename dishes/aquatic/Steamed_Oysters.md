# Steamed Oysters

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

* Oysters
* Scallion
* Garlic
* Ginger
* Soy Sauce
* Brush

## Calculations

* Drinking Water 1 Liter
* Oysters 6
* Scallion 3 stalks
* Garlic 6 cloves
* Ginger 1 small piece
* Soy Sauce 1 ml per oyster

## Operation

* Brush Oysters clean with brush (Use toothbrush if no brush).
* Put water in steamer, after putting steamer rack on, spread 6 Oysters flat on steamer rack, use 50% power, steam 3 minutes.
* Use right hand holding wet cloth to lift hot pot lid, open half of each oyster shell and remove, convex side of oyster downwards, flat side upwards, put 1 ginger shred and 10g garlic mince on each oyster.
* Close hot pot lid, 100% power steam 3.5 minutes.
* Stop fire, use right hand holding cloth to lift hot pot lid, put 5ml sauce on each.
* Plate.
