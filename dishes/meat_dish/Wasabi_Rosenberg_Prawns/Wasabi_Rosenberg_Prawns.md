# Wasabi Rosenberg Prawns (Jie Mo Luo Shi Xia)

![Wasabi Rosenberg Prawns Finished](./Wasabi_Rosenberg_Prawns.jpg)

This dish can be replaced with any shrimp species, including but not limited to Sand Shrimp, Flower Shrimp, Black Tiger Shrimp, etc. Fresh and fragrant, full of Wasabi flavor, licking fingers after eating, and operation is very simple.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Rosenberg Prawns (Giant River Prawn)
- Garlic
- Green Wasabi
- Light Soy Sauce
- Starch
- Pepper Powder
- White Sugar
- Oyster Sauce
- Salt
- Millet Chili (Optional if not eating spicy or replace with Red Bell Pepper)
- Butter
- Cooking Oil

## Calculations

Per portion (About 2 person portion):

- Rosenberg Prawns 250g
- Garlic 1-2 bulbs
- Green Wasabi 20g, for bowl sauce
- Starch 10g, for bowl sauce
- Light Soy Sauce 30g, for bowl sauce
- Pepper Powder 5g, for bowl sauce
- White Sugar 3g, for bowl sauce
- Oyster Sauce 15g, for bowl sauce
- Salt 3g, for bowl sauce
- Millet Chili 1-2
- Butter 20g
- Cooking Oil 80ml

## Operation

1. **Process Shrimp**:
   - Cut shrimp from back, remove shrimp thread and sand bag, can also cut from abdomen, will expand when fried, finished dish is relatively beautiful
   - Wash clean with clear water, dry moisture then can pat on starch, or not pat

2. **Garlic Mince and Bowl Sauce Processing**:
   - Cut 2 bulbs of garlic into garlic mince
   - Prepare bowl sauce, put in Light Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, White Sugar, Pepper Powder, Salt, squeeze in Wasabi according to personal taste, add clear water to dilute then add Starch to dissolve

3. **Stir-fry Process**:
   - Heat pan pour in cooking oil, roughly cover pan bottom
   - Put in dried Rosenberg Prawns, slowly pan-fry
   - After shrimp oil produces (Manifested as large amount of bubbles in pot), add prepared Garlic Mince and Millet Chili
   - After smelling fragrance of Garlic Mince, add Butter
   - After Butter melts flip uniformly, add prepared bowl sauce
   - Cover pot lid simmer 2 minutes until soup juice thick then serve

## Additional Content

- **Precautions**:
  1. Butter should not be fried too long after entering pot, melt and flip uniformly then cook in bowl juice
