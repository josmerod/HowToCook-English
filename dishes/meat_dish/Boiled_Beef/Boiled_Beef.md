# Boiled Beef (Shui Zhu Niu Rou)

![Boiled Beef](./sznr1.jpg)

Spicy Fresh Fragrant

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Beef
- Bean Sprout
- Egg
- Coriander
- Bean Paste
- Cooking Wine
- Starch
- Dried Chili Powder
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Red Chili
- Oyster Sauce

## Calculations

Determine how many servings you plan to make before cooking each time. One serving is exactly enough for 2 people to eat.

This is amount for one serving:

- Beef 300g
- Bean Sprout 100g
- Egg 1
- Coriander 5 stalks
- Bean Paste 10g
- Cooking Wine 10ml
- Starch 15g
- Dried Chili Powder 5g
- Ginger 20g
- Garlic 3 cloves
- Red Chili 1
- Oyster Sauce 8g

## Operation

- Wash Beef clean cut slices.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr2.jpg)

- Add 15g Ginger shreds, 1 Egg, 15g Starch, 8g Oyster Sauce, 10ml Cooking Wine stir evenly, marinate 15 minutes.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr3.jpg)

- Wash Coriander clean cut well.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr4.jpg)

- Pour oil in pot, add Bean Paste, 5g Ginger shreds, Garlic slices.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr5.jpg)

- Pour in boiling water, boil into red soup.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr6.jpg)

- Wash Bean Sprout clean remove tail root, put into boiling water blanch cooked.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr7.jpg)

- Spread Bean Sprout into bowl bottom.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr8.jpg)

- Put Beef slices one by one into Red Soup, fish out after cooked.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr9.jpg)

- Spread Beef on Bean Sprout, sprinkle Coriander stems.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr10.jpg)

- Sprinkle Coriander leaves, Chili Powder, Chili Circles.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr11.jpg)

- Start another pot burn hot oil, drizzle hot oil on dish, then completed.

![Boiled Beef](./sznr12.jpg)
