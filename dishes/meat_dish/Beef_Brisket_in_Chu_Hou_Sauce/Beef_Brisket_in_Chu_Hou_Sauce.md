# Beef Brisket in Chu Hou Sauce (Zhu Hou Niu Nan)

![Beef Brisket in Chu Hou Sauce Finished](./Beef_Brisket_in_Chu_Hou_Sauce.jpeg)
![Beef Brisket in Chu Hou Sauce with Rice](./Beef_Brisket_with_Rice.jpeg)

Meat fragrant delicious, color attractive, nourishing and strong, excellent texture, drooling Cantonese cuisine. Only need 1 hour with pressure cooker, otherwise need to stew 3 hours.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Stew Pot, Pressure Cooker (Optional, but highly recommended!)
- Beef Brisket (Preferred Boneless Short Rib, part with tendon)
  ![Beef Brisket Part](./Beef_Brisket_Cut.jpeg)
- Chu Hou Paste (Core Sauce), Pixian Bean Paste, Red Fermented Bean Curd, Char Siu Sauce (Optional), Oyster Sauce, Dark Soy Sauce, Light Soy Sauce
- Huadiao Wine, White Liquor
- Fragrant Leaf, Sichuan Peppercorn, Star Anise, Dried Chili, Clove, Licorice, Dried Chili, Millet Chili (Optional), Ginger, Garlic

## Calculations

Below is amount for two people eating:

- Beef Brisket 500-600g
- Ginger 30g
- Garlic Half bulb
- Millet Chili 1 (Adjust amount according to taste)
- Fragrant Leaf 2 slices
- Sichuan Peppercorn 0.5g
- Star Anise 2
- Dried Chili 3 (Adjust amount according to taste)
- Clove 3
- Licorice 2 slices
- Red Fermented Bean Curd 2 blocks
- Pixian Bean Paste 15g
- Rock Sugar 10g
- Huadiao Wine 10g
- White Liquor 20g
- Chu Hou Paste 50g
- Oyster Sauce 20g
- Dark Soy Sauce 5g
- Light Soy Sauce 60g
- Char Siu Sauce 20g

## Operation

- Prepare auxiliary ingredients first:

  - Bowl 1: Cut Ginger into 1.5cm\*1.5cm small blocks, peel Half bulb Garlic, if have Millet Chili cut into 1cm small segments
    ![Bowl 1](./Bowl_1.jpeg)
  - Bowl 2: Put 2 Fragrant Leaves, 0.5g Sichuan Peppercorn, 2 Star Anises, 3 Dried Chilis, 3 Cloves, 2 Licorices
    ![Bowl 2](./Bowl_2.jpeg)
  - Bowl 3: Put 2 blocks Red Fermented Bean Curd
    ![Bowl 3](./Bowl_3.jpeg)
  - Bowl 4: Put 15g Bean Paste
    ![Bowl 4](./Bowl_4.jpeg)
  - Bowl 5: Put 10g Rock Sugar
    ![Bowl 5](./Bowl_5.jpeg)
  - Bowl 6: Pour Huadiao Wine 10g and White Liquor 10g
    ![Bowl 6](./Bowl_6.jpeg)
  - Bowl 7: Add 50g Chu Hou Paste, 20g Oyster Sauce, 20g Char Siu Sauce, 5g Dark Soy Sauce, 60g Light Soy Sauce
    ![Bowl 7](./Bowl_7.jpeg)

- No need to cut Beef, put directly into cold water pot, high heat blanch water, fish out beef when water boils
  ![Beef Brisket Blanching](./Beef_Brisket_Blanching.jpeg)
- Rinse impurities on beef surface, cut into 4cm\*4cm\*4cm large blocks, drain dry water then put into bowl set aside
  ![Beef Brisket Cutting](./Beef_Brisket_Chunks.jpeg)
- High heat, hot pot put oil, pour Bowl 1 (Ginger, Garlic, Millet Chili) into pot, fry aromatic
- Medium-low heat, pour Bowl 2 (Spices), stir-fry evenly, about 30 seconds
- Medium-low heat, put Bowl 3 (Red Fermented Bean Curd), crush Red Fermented Bean Curd with spatula
- Medium-low heat, put Bowl 4 (Bean Paste), stir-fry evenly, about 30 seconds
- Medium-low heat, put Bowl 5 (Rock Sugar), fry until melted
- Medium-low heat, put in Beef Brisket, fry until Beef colored
- High heat, drizzle Bowl 6 (Wine) along pot edge, quickly stir-fry, fry until beef surface slightly scorched brown
- Pour Bowl 7 (Sauce), quickly stir-fry, pay attention to bottom juice, fry until small bubbles keep popping, soup juice slightly thick
  ![Beef Brisket Can Start Stewing Now](./Beef_Brisket_Simmering.jpeg)
- Transfer all ingredients in pot to another stew pot or pressure cooker, add water to cover ingredients
  ![Beef Brisket Entering Pot](./Beef_Brisket_in_Pot.jpeg)
- Choose stewing meat time according to pot used:

  - a. Ordinary Stew Pot: Low heat stew 3 hours
  - b. Pressure Cooker: Press 40 minutes

- Open lid season after time, if not salty enough add Salt or Light Soy Sauce (Add small amount, constantly taste, until suitable), if not sweet enough then same logic add Sugar
- Can fish out Beef Brisket first after adjusting taste
- If want to eat Radish Potato, peel cut into 2cm thick slices pour into pot boil 10 - 15 minutes (Or boil to desired texture), if pressure cooker then add pressure boil 5 minutes
  ![Potato Cutting](./Potato_Slices.jpeg)
- Fish out Radish Potato and Beef Brisket put together after boiling
- Filter soup juice drizzle into bowl
  ![Filter Soup Juice](./Filtering_Soup.jpeg)
