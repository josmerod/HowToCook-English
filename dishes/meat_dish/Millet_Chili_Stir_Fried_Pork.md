# Millet Chili Stir-fried Pork (Xiao Mi La Chao Rou)

⚠️ Note: Not recommended for those with a light diet.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

* Millet Chili (Xiao Mi La)
* Peanut Oil
* Pork Belly / Lean Meat
* Salt
* Light Soy Sauce
* Oyster Sauce
* Garlic
* Ginger
* Doubanjiang (Bean Paste)
* Chicken Essence
* White Sugar

## Calculations

Per serving:

- Millet Chili: 20 pieces, adjust according to personal taste
- Peanut Oil: 20ml
- Pork Belly / Lean Meat: 200g
- Salt: 1-2g
- Light Soy Sauce: 10ml
- Oyster Sauce: 10ml
- Ginger and Garlic: 50g
- Doubanjiang: 10g, adjust according to personal taste
- Chicken Essence: 10g
- White Sugar: 5g

## Operation

* Wash Millet Chili clean, cut diagonally into slightly larger pieces.
* For meat, cut into shreds if you like shreds, slices if you like slices. Pour in seasoning (Light Soy Sauce, Oyster Sauce, Salt) and marinate for 5 minutes.
* Heat pot and pour oil, stir-fry meat first then dish up.
* Explode姜 (Ginger) and Garlic until fragrant, pour in Doubanjiang and stir-fry, pour in cut Millet Chili, then pour in lean meat, stir-fry for a while, put some Light Soy Sauce, Chicken Essence, Salt, Sugar and stir-fry.

## Additional Content

**Seasoning can be added according to your own preference, you can also add Dark Soy Sauce, Dark Soy Sauce makes it more fragrant!**
