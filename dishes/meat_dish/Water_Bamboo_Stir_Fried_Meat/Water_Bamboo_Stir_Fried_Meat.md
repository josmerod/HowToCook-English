# Water Bamboo Stir-fried Meat (Jiao Bai Chao Rou)

Water Bamboo (Jiao Bai) tastes delicious, has certain nutritional value.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

* Water Bamboo
* Lean Meat
* Starch
* Cooking Oil
* Chicken Essence
* Ginger
* Garlic
* Cooking Wine
* Salt

## Calculations

Per serving:

* Water Bamboo 2 sticks
* Lean Meat 100 g
* Starch 15 g
* Cooking Oil 30 ml
* Chicken Essence 5 g
* Ginger 1 slice
* Garlic 1 clove
* Cooking Wine 5 ml
* Salt 2 g

## Operation

### Preparation

* Cut Water Bamboo into slices, thickness 0.5 cm each
* Cut Lean Meat into strips, thickness 0.3-0.5 cm, add Cooking Wine, Starch, Salt, Water to stir
* Cut Ginger into slices, chop Garlic head

### Production

* Start pot boil water, put in Water Bamboo, boil for 60-90 S then take out drain dry
* Start pot, pour in 15 ml Oil, pour in Lean Meat, repeatedly flip fry 60 S take out
* Start pot, pour in 15 ml Oil, pour in Ginger, Garlic flip fry 30S, add Water Bamboo continue flip fry 30 S
* Continue adding Lean Meat flip fry 60 S, add 20 ml Water, add Salt, Chicken Essence then flip fry 60S out of pot
