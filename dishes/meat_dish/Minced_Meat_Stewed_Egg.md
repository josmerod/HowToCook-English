# Minced Meat Stewed Egg (Rou Bing Dun Dan)

Minced Meat Stewed Egg is a traditional Chinese home-style dish, also a very popular rice accompaniment. Beginners only need 20 minutes to complete.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Minced Pork
- Egg
- Cooking Wine
- Light Soy Sauce
- White Pepper Powder
- Sesame Oil

## Calculations

- Minced Pork 300g
- Egg 2
- Cooking Wine 10ml
- Light Soy Sauce 20ml
- White Pepper Powder 5g
- Sesame Oil 10-15ml

## Operation

- Add Minced Pork, Cooking Wine, Light Soy Sauce, White Pepper Powder, one Egg and Sesame Oil into bowl, stir evenly.
- Spread seasoned Minced Pork in plate, dig a hole in middle of minced meat with spoon, crack 1 Egg into hole.
- Add water to pot to 1/4 height, after water boils, put plate into pot, cover pot lid, steam for 15 minutes.

## Additional Content

- When taking out Minced Meat Stewed Egg, pay attention plate is very hot, please operate carefully.
