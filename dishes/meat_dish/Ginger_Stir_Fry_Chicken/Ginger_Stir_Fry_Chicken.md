# Ginger Stir-fry Chicken (Jiang Chao Ji)

![Ginger Stir-fry Chicken](./Ginger_Stir_Fry_Chicken.jpg)

Ginger Stir-fry Chicken is a Hunan flavor dish, 5-star for going with rice. Ingredients are ordinary and very easy to cook, learn it once.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Chicken
- Ginger
- Beer
- Light Soy Sauce
- Dark Soy Sauce
- Salt
- Millet Chili
- Beauty Chili (Mei Ren La - Red Chili)
- Pickled Chili (Pao Jiao)
- Garlic

## Calculations

- Chicken = Half (Local chicken/Free-range chicken is best, estimated 650g)
- Cooking Oil = 50ml (Tea oil is best, rapeseed oil if not available)
- Ginger = Half Jin (250g)
- Beer = Half bottle (250ml)
- Light Soy Sauce = 20ml
- Dark Soy Sauce = 10ml
- Salt = 3g
- Millet Chili = 0-5 (0-50g) (Adjust according to spicy taste)
- Beauty Chili = 0-5 (0-50g) (Can use Millet Chili if not available)
- Pickled Chili = 5 (50g)
- Garlic = 3 heads (50g)

## Operation

- Chop chicken into 1cm small pieces as much as possible, drain dry after washing, then marinate with Light Soy Sauce and Cooking Wine for 30 minutes.
- Heat pot on high heat until slightly smoking, put in cooking oil, wait 5 seconds.
- Put in ginger slices then turn to medium heat and stir-fry for 30 seconds.
- Put in chicken pieces and stir-fry for 3 minutes, dry the moisture, fry out chicken oil.
- Put in chopped chilies and garlic cloves, add salt and Dark Soy Sauce, continue stir-frying for 30 seconds.
- Pour in beer, braise on medium-low heat for 2 minutes.
- High heat to reduce sauce (thickening), serve.
