# Fried Dace with Indian Lettuce (Dou Chi Ling Yu You Mai Cai)

![Fried Dace with Indian Lettuce Finished](./Fried_Dace_With_Indian_Lettuce.jpg)

Fried Dace with Indian Lettuce is a very common dish. The ingredients are simple, the operation is convenient, and the dace is salty and fragrant, which goes very well with rice.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Indian Lettuce (You Mai Cai)
- Canned Fried Dace with Salted Black Beans (Gan Zhu Brand recommended)
- Garlic
- Light Soy Sauce
- White Sugar
- Cooking Oil

## Calculations

Per serving (About for 2 people):

- Indian Lettuce: 500g
- Canned Dace: 1 can (About 250g)
- Garlic: 4 cloves
- Light Soy Sauce: 20g
- White Sugar: 3g
- Cooking Oil: 15ml

## Operation

1. **Ingredients Preparation**:
   - Wash Indian Lettuce and cut into segments.
   - Open Dace can, remove main bone of dace, cut into small segments, set aside.
   - Minced Garlic.

2. **Stir-frying Process**:
   - Heat pot and pour cooking oil. After oil is hot, add washed Indian Lettuce, stir-fry until withered and water comes out, then dish out and drain the water produced during frying.
   - Heat pot again, add oil from the Dace can, put in minced garlic to explode fragrance.
   - After garlic is fragrant, add salted black beans from the can, flip mix, then add chopped dace pieces, flip mix again.
   - After mixing evenly, add Indian Lettuce, add Light Soy Sauce and Sugar to season.
   - After seasoning, simply flip mix and dish out.

## Additional Content

- **Attentions**:
  1. Canned Dace has its own taste, adding Light Soy Sauce makes it flavorful enough, no need to add salt.
