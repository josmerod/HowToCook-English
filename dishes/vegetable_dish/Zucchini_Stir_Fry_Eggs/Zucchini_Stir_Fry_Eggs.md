# Zucchini Stir-fry Eggs

![Zucchini Stir-fry Eggs](./Zucchini_Stir_Fry_Eggs.jpeg)

Zucchini Stir-fry Eggs is a simple home-style dish. Simple and easy-to-buy ingredients, delicious and goes well with rice.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Zucchini
- Eggs
- Tomato (Optional)
- Edible Salt
- Cooking Oil

## Calculations

Based on portion for 2 people:

- Zucchini: 500g
- Tomato: 100g
- Eggs: 3
- Cooking Oil: 10-20ml
- Edible Salt: 6g

## Operation

- Wash tomato, cut into small pieces, set aside.
- Wash zucchini, cut into diamond shapes with side length of about 4cm, set aside.
- Crack three eggs into a bowl, beat and mix well, set aside.
- Heat pot, put 5ml - 10ml cooking oil in the pot.
- Pour in eggs, keep stir-frying until eggs become solid, use spatula to divide into small pieces then dish out to a bowl, set aside.
- Put 5ml - 10ml cooking oil in the pot, pour in tomato, stir-fry until soft.
- Pour in zucchini and stir-fry evenly together, put in 6g edible salt, turn down the heat and **wait for 4 - 5 minutes**.
- Pour in reserved eggs, stir-fry over medium heat for 15 seconds.
- Turn off heat, serve.
