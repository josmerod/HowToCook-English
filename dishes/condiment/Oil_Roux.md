# Oil Roux (You Su)

Oil Roux is a mixture of flour and hot oil. It is typically applied to flatbreads (laobing) to ensure distinct layers, a crispy exterior, and a soft interior.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Flour
- Oil
- Salt

## Calculations

- Oil = (Number of flatbreads * 10ml)
- Salt = (Number of flatbreads / 2)g
- Flour = (Number of flatbreads / 0.13)g

## Operation

- Place the flour in a small bowl and add salt.
- Add oil heated to 200°C.
- Stir with chopsticks until it forms a smooth, lump-free paste.
