# Garlic Soy Sauce

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Garlic
- White Sesame Seeds
- Peanut Oil
- Soy Sauce
- Small Dipping Dish

## Calculations

- 2 Garlic Cloves
- White Sesame Seeds 5g
- Peanut Oil 15ml
- Soy Sauce 30ml
- 1 Dipping Dish

## Operation

- Smash the garlic cloves.
- Add the soy sauce to the dipping dish.
- Heat peanut oil in a pan until smoking hot. Add the smashed garlic and fry for 30 seconds.
- Turn off the heat and pour the hot oil and garlic into the dipping dish. Stir well.
