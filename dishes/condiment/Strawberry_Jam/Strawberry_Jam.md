# Strawberry Jam

You can buy small strawberries sold in bulk; they are usually cheaper. Making them into jam to spread on bread is delicious.

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Strawberries
- White Granulated Sugar
- Plastic Wrap

## Calculations

Per portion:

- Strawberries 1200g
- White Sugar 400g (Can be reduced to 200g for a low-sugar version)

## Operation

- Wash the strawberries and remove the leaves.
- Chop the strawberries and place them in a suitable bowl.
- Add the sugar to the bowl and mix well with the strawberries.
- Cover with plastic wrap and let sit for 1 hour.
- Pour the mixture into a non-stick pan and bring to a boil over high heat.
- Once boiling, turn to low heat and stir constantly until the jam becomes thick and viscous.
- Turn off the heat. Let it cool before transferring to sterilized glass jars.
