## Operation

- Add following ingredients, pay attention do not exceed 3/4 of container
  - Dig half egg size Butter put into container, heat **15 seconds** in microwave until melted
  - Melt/Crush **Chocolate/Banana** into **Granule/Paste**
  - Break in an egg and beat scatter stir
  - 15g White (Brown) Sugar (Sweet tooth can add more) (See Appendix 4)
  - Add 1g Salt (**If subsequent taste ingredients contain salt need discretionary**)
  - Add 2.5g Baking Powder
  - Add 15g Flour (Gluten degree no big influence)
  - **Add any liked taste ingredients!** (Nuts and Biscuits etc. dry ingredients not put now)
  - Stir until cannot see dry powder thick yogurt state
  - Add optional dry ingredients (Not including brewing powder) on top
- Praise yourselfğŸ¥°
- Microwave (High Heat) heat **1 Minute** (Until fluffy cake form)
