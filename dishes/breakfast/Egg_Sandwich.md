## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Egg
- Toast
- Bacon
- Butter
- Mayonnaise
- Salt
- Black Pepper

## Calculations

- Egg 1
- Toast 2 Slices
- Bacon 2 Slices
- Butter 10 g
- Mayonnaise 20g
- Salt 1g
- Black Pepper 2g

## Operation

- Cut off four sides of Toast, set aside
- Boil Egg cooked, mash
- Mix Egg, Mayonnaise, Salt, Black Pepper
- Add Butter into pot, fry Bacon cooked
- Assemble Toast, add made Egg Sauce and Bacon between two slices of Toast
- Cut quadrilateral Toast into triangle plate

## Additional Content

- Can also add Cheese, Pickled Cucumber etc. ingredients
