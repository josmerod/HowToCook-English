# Salmon Rice Cooker Rice

![Salmon Rice Cooker Rice Sample](./Salmon_Rice_Cooker_Rice.webp)

Estimated Cooking Difficulty: ★★

## Essential Ingredients and Tools

- Salted Butter
- Salmon
- Rice
- Corn (Optional)
- Enoki Mushroom (Optional)
- Shiitake Mushroom (Optional)

### Tools

- Rice Cooker

## Calculations

- Rice 50g / Person
- Salmon 300g / Person
- Water
- Butter 1 tbsp / Person

## Operation

- De-scale and de-bone the salmon.
- Chop Enoki and Shiitake mushrooms.
- Wash rice three times.
- Put salmon, rice, and butter into the rice cooker.
- For a richer taste, you can add an extra tablespoon of butter.
- Add water according to the rice cooker's scale.
- Set the rice cooker to "Rice" mode and wait approximately 30 - 45 minutes.
