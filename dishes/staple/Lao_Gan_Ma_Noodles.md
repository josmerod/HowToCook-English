## Essential Ingredients and Tools

* Noodles
* Lao Gan Ma (Chili Oil Sauce)
* Soy Sauce

## Calculations

Determine the number of portions before each preparation. One portion is enough for 1 person.

Total:

* 1 liter of water
* 120g of noodles * portion count
* 15ml of Lao Gan Ma * portion count
* 5ml of soy sauce * portion count

## Operation

* Pour water into the pot and bring to a boil.
* Add the noodles evenly into the pot.
* Stir during the cooking process to prevent the noodles from sticking together.
* When you can pick up a noodle with chopsticks and it slides off naturally, wait for 30 more seconds before turning off the heat.
* Transfer the noodles into a bowl.
* Add Lao Gan Ma and soy sauce according to the measurements.
* Mix the noodles, Lao Gan Ma, and soy sauce evenly with chopsticks.
* Eat.
